Movie Summary

Inception is a science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows Dom Cobb, a thief skilled in stealing secrets from within dreams, who is given a chance to clear his name by performing an impossible task—planting an idea into someone's mind.

Actors

Starring Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, along with Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Ellen Page, Tom Hardy, Ken Watanabe, and Cillian Murphy.
